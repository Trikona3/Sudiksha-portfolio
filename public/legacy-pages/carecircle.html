CARE CIRCLE

Case Study

Industry
Health-monitoring
Role
Research, Wireframing, Interface Design
Tools
Figma, Canva
Figma Link

CareCircle is a unified caregiving platform that empowers elderly users to manage medication schedules, track vital signs, and maintain social connections—all from one simple app. It offers automatic refill reminders, integration with health-monitoring devices, a one-tap SOS emergency button, and built-in messaging to stay in touch with family and friends.

A dedicated caregiver portal and real-time dashboard give remote caregivers and healthcare providers visibility into adherence, health metrics, and emergency alerts, while large icons, high-contrast text, and an intuitive layout ensure accessibility for seniors.

Target Audience

Elderly Users

Seniors seeking an easy, reliable way to remember medications, monitor their health, and stay socially connected without tech overwhelm.

Family Caregivers

Relatives who need remote visibility into loved ones’ medication adherence, vitals, and emergency alerts to provide timely support.

Healthcare Professionals

Nurses, doctors, and home-care agencies requiring real-time reports on patient vitals, adherence trends, and emergency events.

User Research

Qualitative research

We conducted semi-structured interviews with seven stakeholders—elderly users, family caregivers, and healthcare professionals—to understand their workflows, pain points, and desired features for a unified caregiving app.

Below are the core questions:

  • How do you currently manage medication schedules and track vital signs?
  • What challenges have you faced with your existing health or caregiving apps?
  • Which features (e.g., refill reminders, SOS button, device integration) matter most to you?
  • How do you prefer to receive alerts about missed doses or emergencies?
  • What would make the app more accessible and easier for seniors to use?

Key insights

  • Seniors need large, high-contrast UI elements and minimal navigation steps.
  • Caregivers value real-time notifications and a remote dashboard to monitor adherence.
  • Healthcare professionals require historical trend data and exportable reports.
  • Users want a reliable one-tap SOS feature tied to their emergency contacts.
  • Seamless integration with wearables and automatic refill reminders boosts trust and compliance.

Design Decisions

Senior-first accessibility baseline

Prioritized larger type, stronger contrast, and fewer navigation steps for elderly users with low digital confidence.

Caregiver visibility and adherence tracking

Designed remote monitoring patterns with timely reminders and status visibility to reduce manual follow-up.

One-tap emergency action

Kept SOS interaction fast and obvious to support high-stress moments without deep navigation.

Integrated health routine support

Emphasized refill reminders, vitals tracking, and connected-device compatibility to improve day-to-day compliance.
